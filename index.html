Role: Team member

Title: LIP READING SYSTEM

Description: Artificial Intelligence is widely used to detect the movement of lips. The visual motion of mouth and corresponding audio data generated are highly correlated. This fact has been exploited for lip reading and for improving speech recognition. A CNN shall detect the movement of lips and judge the words spoken. This trained CNN detects the words that have been spoken in the video and displayed in the text format. The CNN also relies on information provided by the context, knowledge of the language, and any residual hearing. The aim is to verify whether the use of artificial intelligence methods, namely Deep Neural Network, is a suitable candidate for solving this problem. In the practical part, the focus is on presenting the results in terms of the accuracy of the trained neural network on test data.
